Attendance records are retained for as long as your institution’s policy requires or until deleted by an administrator. Backups are kept for disaster recovery with limited access.

We do not sell personal data. Where we use processors (e.g., cloud hosting), they are bound by data protection agreements. Cross‑border transfers, if any, use appropriate safeguards.

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your data, and to object or restrict certain processing. Requests can be sent to brent@senthumolbusiness.co.za.

Security

Transport encryption (HTTPS), role‑based access controls, and audit logging help protect data. No method is 100% secure, but we strive for industry best practices.
